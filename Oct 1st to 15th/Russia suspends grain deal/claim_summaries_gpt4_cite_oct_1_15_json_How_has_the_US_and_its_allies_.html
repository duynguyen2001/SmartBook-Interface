                # How has the US and its allies responded to Russia's suspension of the grain deal and the accusations of sponsoring terrorism?

                # Summary

                The United States Agency for International Development has expressed concern regarding Russia's suspension of the Black Sea grain deal, highlighting the global need for Russia to not use food as a weapon of war . NATO has been focusing on increasing support for Ukraine, with air defenses as a top priority, in response to the ongoing conflict and in light of Russia's actions . Ukrainian President Zelenskiy has called for more stringent sanctions against Russia's energy sector from the G7 and has emphasized the necessity for enhanced air defense capabilities to mitigate Russian aerial attacks . The UN Secretary-General has also shown deep concern about the suspension of the grain deal, urging for its renewal and full implementation to ensure the continued export of food and fertilizer from Ukraine and to remove obstacles to Russian exports .
