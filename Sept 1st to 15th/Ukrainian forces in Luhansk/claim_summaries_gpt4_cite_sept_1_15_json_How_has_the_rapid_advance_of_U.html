                # How has the rapid advance of Ukrainian forces towards Lysychansk affected the morale and position of the Russian forces and their allied militia?

                # Summary

                The rapid advancement of Ukrainian forces has reclaimed an estimated 8,000 square kilometres of northeastern territory from Russian forces, dealing a significant blow to Russian morale and military ambitions . This decisive victory, including the retaking of the Kharkiv region, has been a rout for the Russians, leading to military intelligence failures and soldiers abandoning large amounts of equipment . The Ukrainian counteroffensive along the Oskil River has left Russian forces weakened and without reinforcements, leaving behind heavy weaponry in their retreat . This swift advance by Ukrainian forces, the most significant since March, has unraveled the gains Russia had made, significantly impacting Russia's overall operational design and bringing Ukrainian forces within 50 km of the Russian border .
