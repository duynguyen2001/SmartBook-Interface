                # Who are the potential alternative oil partners that Russia might trade with if a price cap is implemented?

                # Summary

                If a price cap is implemented, Russia has indicated plans to trade with alternative oil partners, though specific countries were not named . Countries that refuse to abide by the G7 price cap, such as China and India, could continue to be recipients of Russian oil . Prior to the invasion, India had imported almost no Russian oil , suggesting a potential expansion of trading relationships. However, redirecting oil supplies to alternative customers like China is a complex task, with significant logistical challenges such as distance and the need for new infrastructure .
