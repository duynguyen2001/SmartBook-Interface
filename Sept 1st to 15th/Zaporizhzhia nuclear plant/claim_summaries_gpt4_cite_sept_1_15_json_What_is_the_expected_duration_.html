                # What is the expected duration of the IAEA team's stay at the Zaporizhzhia nuclear plant, and what factors could influence this timeline?

                # Summary

                The IAEA team's stay at the Zaporizhzhia nuclear plant was initially expected to last "a few days," with the aim to prevent a nuclear accident . This mission was extended when two IAEA experts remained at the plant indefinitely after the initial visit . The length of their stay could be influenced by their ability to establish a "nuclear safety and security protection zone" around the plant, as recommended by the IAEA . The team's work and the duration of their stay may also be affected by whether they can carry out their responsibilities without hindrance and maintain communication, as stressed by Guterres .
