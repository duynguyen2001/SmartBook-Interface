                # What is the significance of Iran's alleged involvement in supplying kamikaze drones to Russia?

                # Summary

                Iran's alleged supply of Shahed-136 kamikaze drones to Russia marks a significant development in the ongoing conflict, as it suggests a form of military partnership between Tehran and Moscow, with Iran providing not only the weaponry but also technical support for their operation . The drones have been used by Russia in attacks against Ukraine, targeting infrastructure and civilians, with claims from Kyiv of around 400 drones already deployed from an order of approximately 2,000 . Despite Iran's denial of involvement, evidence and statements from U.S. officials and Ukrainian President Zelensky indicate that Iran has indeed supplied these drones to Russia, which could potentially alter the dynamics of the conflict if used in larger numbers or in strategic ways . Moreover, the Kremlin's denial of using Iranian drones contrasts with reports from Iranian officials and diplomats, who have acknowledged promises to deliver more drones and missiles to Russia, further implicating Iran in the escalation of the conflict .
