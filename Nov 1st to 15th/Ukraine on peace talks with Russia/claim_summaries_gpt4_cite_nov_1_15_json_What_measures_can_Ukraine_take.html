                # What measures can Ukraine take to ensure that discussions of peace talks do not weaken its residents' resolve or its military efforts?

                # Summary

                Ukraine maintains a firm stance that peace talks can only commence with the restoration of its territorial integrity and the withdrawal of Russian forces . President Zelenskyy has legislated against direct talks with Putin, reflecting a commitment to Ukrainian sovereignty and justice for war crimes . To ensure discussions of peace talks do not undermine Ukraine's position, the country seeks international support, including increased air defense, fighter jets, and calls for a NATO summit to prevent escalation . Meanwhile, emphasizing the non-negotiable terms for negotiations serves as a strategy to preserve the morale and focus of the Ukrainian people and their military .
